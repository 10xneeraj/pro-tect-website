Founder & CEO

Idy (Idora) Marcus

Idy Marcus founded Pro-Tect Services after witnessing firsthand the devastating gaps in protection for vulnerable individuals. As a leadership and conflict-resolution expert, she saw how traditional systems ‚Äî restraining orders, police reports, shelters ‚Äî often fail to provide immediate protection when danger is present.

"I watched someone I cared about live in constant fear, knowing that help might arrive too late. That experience changed me. I knew there had to be a better way," Marcus explains. "Pro-Tect is that better way."

Drawing on her background in corporate leadership and her network of U.S. Veterans, Marcus assembled an elite team dedicated to proactive protection. Her vision: an app-enabled service that puts professional security within reach of anyone facing credible threats.
