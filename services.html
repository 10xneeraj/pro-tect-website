Personal Protection

Our flagship service provides 24/7 on-demand close protection for individuals facing domestic violence, stalking, or credible threats. One tap in our app deploys a trained professional to your location.

What's Included:

  • Dedicated protection agent assigned to your case
  • Rapid response to emergency alerts
  • Discreet accompaniment to high-risk activities
  • Safe escort services (home, work, appointments)
  • Threat assessment and safety planning
  • 24/7 secure communication with your agent

Ideal For:

Individuals experiencing harassment, domestic violence survivors, those with active restraining orders, or anyone facing credible personal threats.
